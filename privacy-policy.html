PRIVACY POLICY

Azure Tower

Last updated: March 2026

1. General Information

This Privacy Policy explains how Azure Tower ("Company", "we", "our", "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data of website visitors, potential buyers, investors, and business partners.

We are committed to processing personal data in accordance with:

  • The Law of Georgia on Personal Data Protection (2023 edition)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR), where applicable
  • International best practices in data security

2. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for personal data processing is:

Azure Tower / MC Construction
Batumi, Georgia
Email: info@azuretower.ge

3. What Personal Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

3.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • WhatsApp / Telegram contact
  • Investment preferences
  • Messages submitted via contact forms

3.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited
  • Time and date of visit
  • Referring website

3.3 Marketing & Analytics Data

If enabled:

  • Google Analytics data
  • Meta (Facebook) Pixel data
  • CRM interaction records

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data on the following legal grounds:

  • User consent
  • Pre-contractual measures (when a user requests project information)
  • Contract performance
  • Legitimate interest (marketing, analytics, business operations)
  • Compliance with legal obligations

5. Purpose of Data Processing

Personal data is processed for:

  • Providing project information and presentations
  • Responding to inquiries
  • Managing sales communications
  • Sending updates regarding launch and availability
  • Investor communication
  • Marketing and remarketing activities
  • Improving website performance and user experience
  • Legal and regulatory compliance

6. Data Storage & Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL)
  • Restricted access controls
  • Role-based data permissions
  • Secure hosting environments
  • Regular monitoring and security review

Personal data is stored only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy or as required by law.

7. International Data Transfers

As part of our operations, personal data may be processed or stored outside Georgia, including within the European Union or other jurisdictions where our service providers operate.

Where required, we ensure appropriate safeguards, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • GDPR-compliant service providers
  • Secure cloud infrastructure

8. Third-Party Service Providers

We may share personal data with trusted third parties, including:

  • CRM systems
  • Marketing platforms
  • Analytics providers
  • Legal and financial advisors
  • Hosting providers

All third-party processors are contractually obligated to maintain data confidentiality and security.

9. Your Rights

Under Georgian law and GDPR (where applicable), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict processing
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise your rights, contact: info@azuretower.ge

10. Cookies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and analyze website performance. Please review our separate Cookie Policy for detailed information.

11. Marketing Communications

You may opt out of marketing communications at any time by:

  • Clicking the unsubscribe link in emails
  • Contacting us directly

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

12. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
