About Azure Tower

Azure Tower. A New Vertical Standard.

Azure Tower is a premium mixed-use development in the heart of Batumi, combining luxury residences, a world-class hotel, modern business spaces, and lifestyle infrastructure. Designed as an architectural landmark, it redefines coastal living through scale, location, and integrated functionality.

The Address Defines the Asset.

Set within Batumi’s elite coastal corridor, alongside internationally recognized hotel brands, Azure Tower benefits from an already established premium ecosystem.
